With over ten years of experience in the field of writing, I’ve polished my command of the English language and communication through various publications. I’ve ghostwritten hundreds of articles for clients including JD Power, The University of Southern California, and Digital Trends. I’m also a current member of the Society for Technical Communication (STC).

So, I began my journey with the ten-month Full-Stack Development Bootcamp through MIT (focusing on the MERN stack) in January of 2021. I’m eager to find a company where I can contribute my coding education along with my communication skills in a junior developer role.
